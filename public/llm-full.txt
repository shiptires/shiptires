## Tire Categories

### All-Season
Best for year-round driving in moderate climates. Balanced wet/dry performance, longer tread life.

### Winter / Snow
Best for cold weather, snow, ice, temperatures below 45°F. Soft rubber compound, 3PMSF certified.

### Performance
Best for sports cars, spirited driving. Maximum grip, responsive handling, higher speed ratings.

### All-Terrain
Best for trucks/SUVs, on/off-road mix. Aggressive tread, sidewall protection.

### Touring
Best for sedans, comfort driving. Smooth ride, low noise, extended mileage warranties.

### Mud-Terrain
Best for serious off-road use. Extreme traction on rocks, mud, trails.

### Highway
Best for SUVs/crossovers on pavement. Quiet, comfortable, long tread life.
